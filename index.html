About Us

Nordic Lanka is a mobile service company based in Aalborg specializing in home and office cleaning as well as car detailing. We are trusted for our quality, reliability, and customer convenience.

Our team of experienced professionals brings the highest standards of Scandinavian quality and attention to detail to every project, ensuring your spaces are not just clean, but hygienically pristine.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive cleaning solutions tailored to your specific needs, ensuring your spaces shine with Nordic quality.

General Cleaning

Our general cleaning service includes thorough vacuuming, dusting, mopping, and surface cleaning to keep your spaces fresh and hygienic.

  • Vacuuming all floors
  • Dusting all surfaces
  • Mopping floors
  • Bathroom & kitchen cleaning

Deep Cleaning

Thorough cleaning ideal for move-ins, post-stays, or long closures. Includes appliance cleaning and sanitizing.

  • Appliance interior cleaning
  • Cabinet interiors
  • Sanitizing kitchen & bathroom
  • Complete disinfection

Office Cleaning

Keep your business space professional and inviting with our tailored office cleaning services.

  • Workspace sanitization
  • Kitchen & break area cleaning
  • Meeting room preparation
  • Floor maintenance
